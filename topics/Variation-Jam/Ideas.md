Ideas for Variations:

-Allegory of The Frog

A play on Plato's "Allegory of The Cave". The game would follow the same philosophy of the book, wherein there are perceived flies and true flies. 

-Anti Aircraft Frog

A game in which the frog is an anti-aircraft gun, and the user must shootdown planes
